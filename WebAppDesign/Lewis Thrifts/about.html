About Elom

Elom is a Christian and Republican YouTuber who loves sharing her passion for thrifting and shopping with her audience. She believes in finding high-quality, stylish items at affordable prices, and wants to help others do the same. Through her videos, Elom hopes to inspire and empower women to feel confident in their personal style.

When she's not thrifting, Elom enjoys spending time with her family and friends, reading, and volunteering at her local church.
